Part 6 — Auto Spin Wheel

Auto Spin Wheel

1

Templates are built in

Auto Spin Wheel is detection-based, and the templates it needs ship built in — nothing to capture, works out of the box.

2

Pick the wheel type and go

On the tab, choose Super Wheelspin (3 prizes at once) or normal Wheelspin (1 prize). In-game, open the My Horizon menu so the matching tile is visible. If you captured the My Horizon tab template, you can also start from the main menu — FAFE clicks the tab for you.

3

Choose duplicate handling

On the tab, pick Add to Garage (keep) or Sell. ⚠ Sell mode sells duplicate cars automatically and unattended — use with care.

4

Set the count and start

Set the number of spins (0 = unlimited, stop with F9). Open 🎡 Auto Spin Wheel in the sidebar, then click ▶ Start or press F9. The countdown is 3 seconds; with background mode enabled, you do not need to switch back to Forza.

5

Fully automatic

FAFE clicks the chosen Wheelspin tile to start, then every spin auto-continues: detect the prompt → (optional) skip → Enter collects the prize (all 3 at once for a Super Wheelspin) → handle 0–3 duplicate cars. FAFE never presses to spin itself.

6

Stop

Press F9 or click ■ Stop anytime. With a set count, the final spin collects with Esc and ends back on the My Horizon menu.
