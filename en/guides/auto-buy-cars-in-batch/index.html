Part 4 — Auto Buy Cars in Batch

Auto Buy Cars in Batch

Use FAFE to repeatedly purchase any car that is available to buy. Choose the automatic 22B-STI preset or manually open the car you want first.

1

Open Buy Cars

Click 🛒 Buy Cars in the FAFE sidebar.

2

Choose how to position the car

Main-menu preset (22B-STI only): Leave Forza on the main menu. FAFE automatically navigates through Collection Journal → Discover Japan / Master Explorer → Car Collection → Subaru, opens the 22B-STI, and returns to the main menu when finished.

Manual positioning (any purchasable car): Open Collection Journal → Car Collection yourself, select the car you want to buy, click it, and leave the game on its detail/purchase screen. FAFE will repeat the purchase sequence for that car.

3

Choose the batch size and start

Set how many cars to buy (0 = unlimited), then click ▶ Start or press F9. The countdown is 3 seconds; with background mode enabled, you do not need to switch back to Forza.

4

Let FAFE repeat the purchase

FAFE presses Space → Down → Enter → Enter → Enter, with 0.5 seconds between inputs by default, then repeats until the batch is complete. You can adjust the interval in Settings.

5

Stop

Press F9 or click ■ Stop at any time to stop cleanly.

Remember: the buying macro works with any purchasable car when you position it manually. Only automatic navigation from the main menu is preset for the 22B-STI.
