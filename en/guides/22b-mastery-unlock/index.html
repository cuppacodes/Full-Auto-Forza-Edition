Part 3 — Auto Unlock 22B Wheels

Auto Unlock 22B Wheels

1

Clear the car path (no deleting)

The tool walks your selected range of cars top-to-bottom, column by column. Just make sure that range is all the 22B-STis you want to unlock — don't leave other cars in the path. You don't need to delete other cars from your garage.

2

Sort by Recently Added

In the garage screen press X to open the sort menu and select Recently Added. The tool re-sorts after each car automatically.

3

Stop at the My Home garage screen

Leave the game on the My Home garage screen (not the Horizon Festival map menu), with the first car you want to unlock in the top-left (newest) position.

My Home garage ready — first car in the top-left position, all cars are 22B-STi
4

Set the unlock path & car range

In the function panel: use the unlock-path grid to tick the mastery-tree nodes to unlock (the tool walks them with WASD+Enter — no node capture), and the garage block to pick the contiguous range of cars — first row, middle full columns, last row, matching the top-to-bottom blocks in your garage.

5

Press Start

Open the ⭐ Unlock Spin Wheel function in the sidebar, click ▶ Start or press F9, and switch back to Forza during the countdown.

6

Fully automatic from here

The tool enters each car top-to-bottom, opens Car Mastery, unlocks the nodes by keyboard, returns to the garage, and moves to the next — through your whole selected range.
