Part 4 — Auto Buy 22B-STi

Auto Buy 22B-STi

1

Open Buy Cars

Click 🛒 Buy Cars in the sidebar.

2

Position on the 22B-STi (auto or manual)

Just stay on the main menu — FAFE walks Collection Journal → Discover Japan / Master Explorer → Car Collection → Subaru, opens the 22B-STi, and returns to the main menu when done. Or position manually: Menu → Collection Journal → Master Explorer → Car Collection → click the 22B-STi and leave the game on that screen.

3

Press Start

Back in FAFE, choose how many to buy (0 = unlimited), then click ▶ Start or press F9. Switch back to Forza during the 5-second countdown.

4

Fully automatic purchasing

The tool presses: Space → Down → Enter → Enter → Enter with 0.5s between each input (adjustable in Settings), completing one purchase then repeating.

5

Stop

Press F9 or click ■ Stop at any time to stop cleanly.

✦

Extra — it's just a key macro

This feature is fundamentally just a key macro, so it works for any car you can buy, not only the 22B-STi.
