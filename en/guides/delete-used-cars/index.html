Part 5 — Delete Used Cars

Delete Used Cars

1

Prepare your garage

Make sure the deletion path contains only cars you want to delete. The tool starts from the car currently being viewed and deletes toward newer cars, one by one — it can't skip specific ones.

2

Sort, and stop on your starting car

Set the garage sort to Newest first, then go to the oldest car you want to start from. For example, with 12 cars, navigate to the 12th and leave the screen viewing that car.

3

Open Delete Cars & set the range

Click 🗑️ Delete Cars in the sidebar and use the garage block to pick the contiguous range of cars to delete (first row, middle full columns, last row) — the grid shows the total count. Then tick the "I understand all cars between the first and last car will be deleted" box to enable Start.

4

Press Start

Back in FAFE, click ▶ Start or press F9, and switch to Forza during the 5-second countdown.

5

Fully automatic

The tool presses: Enter → Down ×4 → Enter → Down ×1 → Enter. After each deletion the game selects the next car automatically.

6

Stop

Press F9 or click ■ Stop at any time.
